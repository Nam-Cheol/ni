# Actors and outcomes

## Actors

- Traveler: shares trip intent, constraints, preferences, and unresolved needs.
- Human concierge: receives the handoff brief and performs any real-world follow-up.
- Planning model: may draft or revise the protocol but cannot declare readiness.
- CLI: validates readiness and lock state.

## Outcomes

- Traveler receives a bounded intake conversation, not booking or regulated advice.
- Human concierge receives a structured brief with facts, constraints, risks, and next action.
- Planning owner can prove the non-software product is locked before downstream work begins.
