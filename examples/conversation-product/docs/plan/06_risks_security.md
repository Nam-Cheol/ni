# Risks and security

## RISK-001: Unsupported certainty

Severity: high

Mitigation: use provisional language, require human verification, and evaluate transcripts for claims about bookings, availability, safety, or regulated advice.

## RISK-002: Sensitive data collection

Severity: medium

Mitigation: collect only triage data, avoid regulated advice, and include privacy reminders in human handoff material.

## RISK-003: Weak handoff quality

Severity: medium

Mitigation: evaluate sample briefs against required fields for facts, constraints, open questions, risk notes, and next action.
