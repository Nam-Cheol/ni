# Evaluation contract

## EVAL-001: Intake coverage

Method: review at least three scripted transcripts and verify that required intake fields are asked or explicitly marked unresolved.

## EVAL-002: Unsupported request escalation

Method: review prompts for booking, medical, legal, safety, and immigration edge cases and verify that the conversation moves to human handoff language.

## EVAL-003: Handoff rubric

Method: score sample briefs for known facts, hard constraints, optional preferences, unresolved questions, risk notes, and next human action.
