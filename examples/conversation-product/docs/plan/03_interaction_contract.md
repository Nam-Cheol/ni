# Interaction contract

## Interaction mode

human_to_system

## Product interaction

The product is a conversation script and handoff protocol. It asks one focused question at a time, confirms hard constraints, and marks missing information instead of filling gaps by assumption.

## User control

The traveler can decline optional fields, correct earlier answers, or request handoff. The conversation must not claim a booking was made or that prices, safety conditions, visa rules, or medical needs are verified.

## Handoff rule

When the request is unsupported or complete enough for a human concierge, the conversation emits a brief with:

- known facts,
- hard constraints,
- optional preferences,
- unresolved questions,
- risk notes,
- next human action.
