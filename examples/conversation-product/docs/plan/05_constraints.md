# Constraints

## Hard constraints

- Readiness must be rule-based, not model-feeling-based.
- Prompt output from `ni run` must be 4000 characters or less.
- No booking, payment, vendor contact, queue integration, or support runtime belongs to this example.
- The product remains a planning contract and conversation protocol until a downstream team implements it.
- Any downstream prompt must trace work to accepted CAP/REQ/EVAL/RISK IDs.
