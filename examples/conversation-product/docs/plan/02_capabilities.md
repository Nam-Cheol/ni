# Capabilities

## CAP-001: Bounded intake

Collect destination, dates or flexibility, budget range, traveler count, accessibility needs, and trip purpose. Keep hard constraints separate from optional preferences.

## CAP-002: Unsupported request handling

Detect booking requests, guarantees, regulated advice, and safety-critical requests. Move to explicit human handoff language without inventing availability or policy.

## CAP-003: Human concierge handoff

Produce a concise brief with known facts, unresolved questions, user constraints, risk notes, and the next human action.
