# Delivery and operation

## Delivery surfaces

- document

## Initial delivery

The initial delivery is locked planning material and generated prompts for a research team or generic downstream worker. It does not include fieldwork, collection, or analysis execution.

## Operating model

- Planning docs are committed to git.
- Contract JSON is committed to git.
- The lockfile is authoritative after `ni end`.
- Generated prompts are derived handoff material and can be regenerated from the lock.
