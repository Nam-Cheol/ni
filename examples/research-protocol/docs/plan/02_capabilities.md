# Capabilities

## CAP-001: Study definition

State the research question and hypothesis in falsifiable terms. Define eligible street segments, observation windows, and exclusion criteria.

## CAP-002: Data and ethics boundaries

Separate public environmental observation from participant contact or personally identifying data. Require ethics review before any human-subject interaction.

## CAP-003: Reproducible analysis plan

Define variables, comparison method, exclusions, reviewer evidence, and acceptance criteria before observations are collected.
