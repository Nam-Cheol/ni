# Risks and security

## RISK-001: Sampling bias

Severity: high

Mitigation: require explicit inclusion and exclusion criteria plus independent reproduction of the sampling frame before fieldwork.

## RISK-002: Ethics boundary breach

Severity: high

Mitigation: separate public environmental observation from participant contact and require ethics review before collecting personal data.

## RISK-003: Analysis drift

Severity: medium

Mitigation: lock variables, comparison method, exclusion criteria, and evidence package before observations are collected.
