# Actors and outcomes

## Actors

- Principal investigator: owns the protocol and approves research boundaries.
- Field researcher: follows the locked sampling and observation plan after separate approval.
- Ethics reviewer: checks participant-contact and privacy boundaries.
- Planning model: may draft protocol text but cannot declare readiness.
- CLI: validates readiness and lock state.

## Outcomes

- Study question, sampling frame, data handling, and analysis plan are explicit.
- Fieldwork is blocked until ethics and protocol review happen outside ni.
- Downstream research team receives a prompt derived from the lock rather than model memory.
