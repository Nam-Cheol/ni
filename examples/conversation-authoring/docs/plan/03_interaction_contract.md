# Interaction contract

## Conversation boundaries

- Ask focused questions when the user provides a vague product idea.
- Treat tentative or incomplete details as assumptions or open questions.
- Convert only confirmed answers into accepted requirements, decisions, or
  non-goals.
- Show a short change summary after model-authored planning edits.

## Refund triage behavior

- Draft recommendation only; never approve or issue a refund.
- Cite ticket facts and the internal refund policy page.
- Escalate ambiguity, policy conflict, missing decisive facts, or exceptions to
  a supervisor.
- Keep customer-contact language out of scope.

## Authority boundary

The planning model maintains docs and contract records. `ni status`, `ni end`,
and `ni run` are the authority for readiness, locking, and prompt compilation.
