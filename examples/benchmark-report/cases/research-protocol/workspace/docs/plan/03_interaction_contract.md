# Interaction contract

## Interaction mode

human_to_human

## Product interaction

The benchmark case is a planning artifact for a future research team handoff,
not a participant-facing research interaction. Any future human-service or
field workflow remains blocked until the research question, scope, consent,
privacy, data handling, accessibility, safety, and review evidence are
accepted.

## User control

The user must answer, defer with reason, mark not applicable, or keep blocking
OQ-001 through OQ-005. The model must not convert vague statements into
accepted study decisions. The CLI remains the readiness authority.
